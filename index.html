A Daughter's request to her Dad

Mere Baba

Mujhe itna pyaar na do Baba,
Kal jitna mujhe naseeb na ho ...
Yeh jo maatha chuma karte hai,
Kal ispar shikanj ajeeb na ho ...
Mai jab bhi roti hu Baba,
Aap aansun pocha karte hai ...
Mujhe itna dur na chhod aana,
Fir mai roun toh aap Qareeb na ho ...

Mere Naaz uthate ho aap,
Mere Laad uthate ho aap,
Meri choti choti Khwahish par Jaan lutaate ho aap ...
Kal aisa na ho dusre ghar mai,
Mai tanha aapko yaad karu,
Aur ro rokar Fariyaad karu ...

Aye Allah!
Mere Baba sa koi pyaar jatane wala ho,
Mere Naaz uthaane wala ho ...

Bas itni si iltejah hai apni
Masoom si beti ke Baba ...
Jisko bhi saupna mujhe,
Bas nek dekhna ...
Bhooki rahungi din bhar,
Bas apne jaisa pyaar dene wala dekhnaa ...

Bas kisi aise Insaan ko saupna,
Jo mujhe Insaan samjhkar rakhe Mere Baba ...
